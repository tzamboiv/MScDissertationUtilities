Milpitas Police Enlist PredPol to Reduce Crime

Posted on Sep 25, 2013 in Dr. Jeff Brantingham, LAPD, Predictive Policing, PredPol, Santa Cruz Police | No Comments

NBC Bay Area did a story on Milipitas Police’s recent deployment of PredPol. Here is part of the text and the link:

Milpitas police have a new way of patrolling the streets thanks to technology.

A new computer program the Milpitas Police Department is using predicts where criminals will strike next in the city. The program, Predpol, analyzes crime data to help determine a prediction.

In addition to the Milpitas, police departments in Santa Cruz and Los Angeles are using the program.

A state grant is paying for the program. Click here for the video.
