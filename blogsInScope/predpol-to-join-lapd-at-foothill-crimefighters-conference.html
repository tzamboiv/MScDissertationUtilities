PredPol to Join LAPD at Foothill Crimefighter’s Conference

Posted on Feb 3, 2013 in Capt. Sean Malinowski, LAPD, Los Angeles Police, Predictive Policing, PredPol, Santa Cruz Police, UCLA | No Comments

PredPol will join with Los Angeles Police, representatives from Santa Cruz Police and mathematicians from UCLA and UC Irvine at the Foothill Crimefighter’s Conference on February 5th. Experts will demonstrate predictive policing and forecasting technology and bring together analysts, officers and others to review what has worked in the last year in the Foothill Division of LAPD – the first division to pilot PredPol for LAPD. The Foothill Division has seen a marked reduction in crime after implementing PredPol and this review will look at ways to improve upon these results using PredPol and analytical methods.
