Former Facebook Chief Privacy Officer Joins PredPol Board

Posted on Feb 13, 2014 in Predictive Policing, PredPol | No Comments

Former Facebook Chief Privacy Officer Chris Kelly has joined the PredPol Board of Directors. As Facebook’s first Chief Privacy Officer, General Counsel, and Head of Global Public Policy, Chris played an integral role in the growth of Facebook. Chris has been a long time supporter of leveraging technology to improve public safety and was an early adviser and supporter of PredPol’s technology and public-focused mission.

Chris has a BA in Government and Philosophy from Georgetown University, a Master of Arts in Political Science from Yale and a JD from Harvard. He is a part owner of the Sacramento Kings professional basketball franchise.
